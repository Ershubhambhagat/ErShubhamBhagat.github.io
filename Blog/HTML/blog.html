BugMeNot

27 June 2022

BugMeNot is an Internet service that provides usernames and passwords to let Internet users bypass mandatory free registration on websites.

Excellent for sites with annoying "login required" to view stuff

Many sites require you to register just to see a simple video or read a review... etc. Even if this registration is free if you stumble on a site that you are unlikely to visit again, creating yet another registration is a haste.BugMeNot.com will help you quickly bypass the login of web sites that require compulsory registration and/or the collection of personal/demographic information. The service is quite good as you only have to enter the site you need to log into and some other user would have shared credentials.
